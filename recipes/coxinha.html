Bodybuilder "Coxinhas"

Coxinha

A pear-shaped, deep-fried chicken filled croquette.

It's very popular in Brazil and can be found at snack bars, bakeries, bars and at parties. This recipe will provide a good amount of nutrients for the gym.

Ingredients

  • Shredded chicken
  • Sweet potato
  • Cream cheese
  • Oatmeal

Steps

  1. Boil the sweet potato.
  2. After boiled add the sweet potato and chicken to a mixer and mix until you got a homogeneous mixture.
  3. With the mixture you got make little "plates" to add the chicken with cream cheese and close to make the shape of a coxinha.
  4. Wrap the coxinha in the oatmeal.
  5. Put it on the air fryer for 6 minutes with 180℃(356℉) and more 10-12 minutes with 200℃(392℉) to make it crunchy.
