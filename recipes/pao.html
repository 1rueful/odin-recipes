Chicken Bread

Low carb bread recipe

You can eat this bread to beat your daily protein.

Ingredients

  • 100g of shredded chicken
  • 1 egg
  • Grated cheese
  • Baker's yeast

Steps

  1. Combine the shredded chicken, egg and a tablespoon of grated cheese in a mixer.
  2. After its mixed add a teaspoon of baker's yeast and mix more.
  3. Add the mix to a bowl with the shape of the bread and put in on the microwave for 2 minutes.
  4. With the bread can cut it on the middle and add something(I like put cheese) and put on the sandwich maker.
