Kibbeh

A torpedo-shaped, deep-fried croquette made with bulgur wheat and ground beef.

Ingredients

  • 150g of minced beef knuckle
  • 100g of cassava(boiled)
  • Quail eggs(boiled)

Steps

  1. Combine the minced beef and cassava in a mixer.
  2. With the homogeneous mixture water your hands and make meat balls with a finger hole to add the quail egg after close the hole and do the kibbeh shape.
  3. You can wrap the kibbeh in oatmeal but that is preference.
  4. Put it on the air fryer for 6 minutes with 180℃(356℉) and more 10-12 minutes with 200℃(392℉) to make it crunchy.
